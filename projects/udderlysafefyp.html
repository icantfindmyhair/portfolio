Udderly Safe

Final year project: Application for cow lameness detection. Selected to be stored in UTAR Institutional Repository. Read it here.

★ Cow lameness - highly treatable locomotive condition but often overlooked
★ Detect cow lameness from a single image using a custom-trained YOLOv8 image processing model.
★ Built in herd management and individual cow health record
★ Cheap and portable solution for a costly disease
